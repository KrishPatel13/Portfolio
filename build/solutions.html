For solutions of extra practice problems:

Follow these steps:

    1. First Check if anyone has not asked about the same question you want to ask on Piazza.

    2. If Not, then create a NEW public piazza post.

    3. Select the Tag: "extra-practice"

    4. Title it in Following format:
        Format: [Extra Practice/[fill in stewart section]/[question number]/[*code*]]: [your question in brief]
        e.g.: [Extra Practice/14.7/Q1/W08A]: Please check if this is correct answer

    5. In the body of the post, write your question in detail. Including the Following:
        > Picture of the Question attached (in-line)
        > [IMPORTANT]: Picture of your work so far or your solution attempt.
        > Your question in text

    6. POST!

Note: 
    > For *code* see the upper right corner of the extra practice pdf.
    > [....] is to be filled by you in title format.

I hope this problems help you to prep for final exams!
